# Fastvid — Design Brainstorm

## Concept
AI-powered YouTube video generation SaaS. One prompt → complete 15–20 min viral YouTube video (script, voiceover, visuals, effects). €500/month, unlimited videos.

## Idea 1 — "Dark Cinematic Studio"
**Design Movement:** Cinematic Dark UI / High-Tech Production House
**Core Principles:**
- Deep blacks and charcoal surfaces evoking a professional video editing suite
- Electric orange/amber as the sole accent — energy, speed, creativity
- Asymmetric layouts with heavy left-anchored text blocks and right-side video mockups
- Brutalist typographic hierarchy: oversized display numbers and labels

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: near-black `#0A0A0B` with subtle warm undertone
- Surface: `#141416` cards with `1px` warm-tinted borders
- Accent: Electric amber `#F59E0B` → `#FBBF24` gradient for CTAs
- Text: Off-white `#F5F5F0` primary, `#888` secondary

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-bleed hero with diagonal split: left = bold headline, right = animated video generation mockup
- Sections alternate between full-width dark panels and contained card grids
- Stats row with massive numerals (e.g. "15–20 min" in 120px font)

**Signature Elements:**
- Glowing amber progress bar / waveform animation in hero
- "Terminal-style" prompt input box with blinking cursor
- Film grain texture overlay at 3% opacity on all dark sections

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states reveal amber glow borders on cards
- CTA buttons pulse subtly on idle
- Scroll-triggered number counters

**Animation:**
- Hero text slides in from left on load (0.6s ease-out)
- Feature cards stagger-fade in on scroll
- Ambient particle drift in hero background

**Typography System:**
- Display: `Syne` (700–800) — geometric, futuristic
- Body: `DM Sans` (400–500) — clean, readable
- Accent labels: `Space Mono` — monospace for technical feel
</text>

## Idea 2 — "Neon Gradient Studio" (SELECTED)
**Design Movement:** Modern SaaS / Glassmorphism with Bold Gradient Accents
**Core Principles:**
- Deep navy-to-black background with vivid violet-to-cyan gradient accents
- Glass-effect cards with frosted blur and subtle borders
- Bold asymmetric hero with floating UI mockup elements
- Clean, airy spacing with strong typographic contrast

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: `#050510` deep space navy
- Gradient accent: `#7C3AED` violet → `#06B6D4` cyan (brand gradient)
- Surface: `rgba(255,255,255,0.04)` glass cards
- Text: Pure white primary, `#94A3B8` muted secondary

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Hero: left-aligned headline + sub + CTA, right side has animated floating "generation in progress" UI card
- Feature section: 3-column asymmetric grid with icon + title + description
- Pricing: centered single card with gradient border glow
- Testimonials: horizontal scroll strip

**Signature Elements:**
- Gradient text on key headline words ("viral", "AI")
- Glowing orb blobs in background (blurred, low opacity)
- Animated generation steps (script → voice → visuals → effects) as a visual timeline

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Buttons have gradient fill with shimmer sweep on hover
- Cards lift with subtle shadow on hover
- Smooth scroll with section fade-ins

**Animation:**
- Background orbs slowly drift
- Hero mockup floats with gentle bob
- Step timeline animates sequentially on scroll

**Typography System:**
- Display: `Clash Display` / `Outfit` (700–800)
- Body: `Inter` (400–500)
- Code/labels: `JetBrains Mono`

## Idea 3 — "Bold Editorial Tech"
**Design Movement:** Editorial Brutalism meets Silicon Valley SaaS
**Core Principles:**
- High contrast black/white base with a single vivid red accent
- Oversized editorial typography dominates every section
- Rigid grid broken intentionally by full-bleed image strips
- Minimal decoration — let the copy and layout do the work

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: Pure white `#FFFFFF`
- Text: Jet black `#0D0D0D`
- Accent: Signal red `#E5001A`
- Dividers: Thick 2px black rules

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Hero: massive 96px+ headline spanning full width, CTA below, no imagery
- Feature section: alternating text-left / image-right rows with thick border separators
- Pricing: large bordered box, no gradients
- FAQ: accordion with bold question text

**Signature Elements:**
- Red underline / highlight on key words
- Section numbers in oversized light-weight type (01, 02, 03)
- Newspaper-column text layout for the "How it works" section

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states invert colors (black bg, white text)
- No rounded corners — sharp edges throughout
- Underline animations on links

**Animation:**
- Text reveals with clip-path wipe on scroll
- No floating elements — everything is grounded
- Subtle parallax on section backgrounds

**Typography System:**
- Display: `Bebas Neue` (400) — tall, impactful
- Body: `Libre Baskerville` (400–700) — editorial serif
- Labels: `Space Grotesk` (500–600)
